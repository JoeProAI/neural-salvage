## üìã **Step-by-Step Instructions**

### **1. View Your NFT**
- Go to "My NFTs" gallery
- Click on the NFT you want to sell
- You'll see the NFT detail page

### **2. Click "List for Sale"**
- Find the cyan "List for Sale" button
- Click it to open the listing modal

### **3. Set Your Price**
- Enter price in USD (e.g., $50)
- The modal will automatically convert to AR tokens
- You'll see the current AR exchange rate

### **4. Choose Duration**
- **7 days** - Good for quick sales
- **30 days** - Standard duration
- **90 days** - Long-term listing
- **No expiration** - Stays listed until sold

### **5. Review Fees**
The modal shows a clear breakdown:
```
Sale Price:       $50.00
Platform Fee (2%): -$1.00
You Receive:      $49.00
```

### **6. Connect Wallet**
- If not connected, click "Connect Wallet"
- Choose your wallet (Wanderer, ArConnect, etc.)
- Authorize the connection

### **7. Sign Transaction**
- Click "List for Sale"
- Your wallet will ask you to sign
- Click "Approve" or "Sign" in your wallet
- **This is FREE - you don't pay gas fees!**

### **8. Wait for Confirmation**
- Transaction submits to Arweave
- You'll see "Listed Successfully!" message
- Your NFT appears on BazAR in 2-3 minutes

## üêõ **Troubleshooting**

### **"Please connect your wallet first"**
- Click "Connect Wallet" button
- Choose your wallet extension
- Authorize connection
- Try listing again

### **"Failed to create listing"**
- Check wallet is connected
- Ensure price is greater than $0
- Try refreshing the page
- Check browser console for errors

### **"User rejected signature"**
- You clicked "Reject" in wallet
- This is normal if you changed your mind
- Click "List for Sale" again to retry

### **Listing doesn't appear on BazAR**
- Wait 5 minutes (blockchain confirmation)
- Clear browser cache
- Check transaction on ViewBlock
- Listing is still valid even if not visible yet

### **Price shows wrong amount**
- AR price fluctuates
- USD ‚Üí AR conversion updates every minute
- Your listing locks in AR amount when created
- USD value will change with market
