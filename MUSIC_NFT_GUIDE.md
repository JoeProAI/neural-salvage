## â“ **FAQ:**

### **Q: Can I mint music I don't own?**
**A:** No! Only mint music you created or have rights to. This is illegal otherwise.

### **Q: What if someone downloads my NFT audio?**
**A:** They can listen, but they don't OWN it. The blockchain proves YOU (the NFT holder) are the official owner. Like how you can listen to Spotify, but you don't own the songs.

### **Q: How do royalties work?**
**A:** When your NFT is resold, you automatically earn 3% of the sale price. Forever. Enforced by blockchain.

### **Q: Can I mint the same song twice?**
**A:** Yes! Create limited editions (e.g., 10 copies) for scarcity.

### **Q: What about copyright?**
**A:** You retain all rights. The NFT is proof of ownership of THAT specific file, not the copyright itself. You can still sell the song on Spotify, iTunes, etc.

### **Q: Can I delete my NFT?**
**A:** No! That's the point. It's PERMANENT. Once minted, it exists forever on Arweave.

### **Q: How do I get paid when it sells?**
**A:** Funds go directly to your connected wallet. Instant. No middleman.
