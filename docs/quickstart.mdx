## Get Started in 5 Minutes

This guide will walk you through minting your first permanent NFT on Neural Salvage.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Visit [Neural Salvage](https://neural-salvage.vercel.app) and sign in with Google.
    
    ```bash
    # No installation required - it's all web-based!
    ```
    
    âœ… Your account is created instantly
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Your Asset">
    Navigate to your dashboard and click **Upload Asset**.
    
    <Info>
      Supported formats:
      - Images: JPG, PNG, GIF, SVG
      - Videos: MP4, MOV, WEBM
      - Audio: MP3, WAV, OGG
    </Info>
    
    Drag and drop your file or click to browse.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mint as NFT">
    Once uploaded, click **Mint as NFT** on your asset.
    
    You'll see:
    - Estimated cost (~$0.50 for typical image)
    - Storage size
    - Blockchain confirmation time
    
    Click **Confirm Mint** to proceed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify on Blockchain">
    After minting completes (usually 30-60 seconds), you'll receive:
    
    - Arweave transaction ID
    - Permanent URL to your NFT
    - Metadata link
    
    View your NFT on [Arweave Explorer](https://viewblock.io/arweave) to verify!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens During Minting?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Asset Upload to Arweave">
    Your file is uploaded to Arweave's permanent storage network via Bundlr.
    
    - **Instant**: Upload completes in seconds
    - **Permanent**: Stored for 200+ years
    - **Decentralized**: Replicated across global nodes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Metadata Creation">
    NFT metadata is created following standard formats:
    
    ```json
    {
      "name": "Your Asset Name",
      "description": "Your description",
      "image": "https://arweave.net/your-asset-id",
      "attributes": [
        {
          "trait_type": "Creator",
          "value": "your-username"
        }
      ]
    }
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Blockchain Confirmation">
    Your NFT is registered on Arweave blockchain:
    
    - Transaction is bundled
    - Submitted to Arweave network
    - Confirmed in ~30-60 seconds
    - Permanent record created
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
